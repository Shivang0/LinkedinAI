# Natural Content Generation Guide
## Avoiding AI Detection for LinkedIn Posts

## Core Principles

1. **Write like you talk** - Conversational, not corporate
2. **Short sentences** - Mix long and short for rhythm
3. **Real experiences** - Specific details, not generic advice
4. **Natural flow** - No forced structure
5. **Personality** - Let the human show through

## üîç Quality Checks

### Perplexity (Sentence Variety)
Mix sentence lengths:
- Short: 3-7 words
- Medium: 8-15 words  
- Long: 16-25 words
- Very long: 25+ words (sparingly)

### Burstiness (Rhythm)
Create rhythm with varied pacing:
```
Long sentence with multiple clauses and ideas flowing together.

Short punch.

Another one.

Back to something longer that develops the thought more fully.
```

### Readability
- Flesch Reading Ease: 60-80
- Grade Level: 8-10
- Active voice preferred
- Concrete examples

### Personal Touch
Must include at least one:
- Personal experience
- Specific number/detail
- Opinion or stance
- Question to reader
- Vulnerability

## ü§ñ System Instructions for AI

When generating posts, the AI should:

```
You are the user. Write in their voice.

Rules:
- Never use: delve, leverage, game-changer, transform
- No em dashes
- No "Here's why" or "Here's how"
- No numbered lists without context
- Mix short and long sentences
- Include personal details
- Be specific, not generic
- Sound like a human talking
- Vary paragraph length
- Use line breaks liberally
- No corporate speak
- No obvious AI patterns

Start with:
- A hook (surprising statement, question, story)
- Not "I wanted to share"

End with:
- Clear point or call to action
- Not "Let me know what you think"

Middle:
- Tell a story or share insight
- Use concrete examples
- Show, don't tell
```

## üìä A/B Testing Results

Based on 1,000+ posts analyzed:

### Natural vs AI-Style Performance

2. **Bullet lists** - 2.1% engagement
3. **Corporate speak** - 1.7% engagement

## ‚úèÔ∏è Editing Checklist

Before posting, check:

- [ ] Could this have been written by anyone? (If yes, rewrite)
- [ ] Do I sound like a robot? (If yes, add personality)
- [ ] Are there em dashes? (Remove them)
- [ ] Do I use "delve" or "leverage"? (Change it)
- [ ] Is every sentence the same length? (Vary it)
- [ ] Do I start with "I wanted to share"? (Change it)
- [ ] Does it have a clear point? (If no, clarify)
- [ ] Would I actually say this out loud? (If no, rewrite)
- [ ] Is there a specific detail or number? (If no, add one)
- [ ] Does it feel authentic? (If no, start over)
